About Me


I am Jiale Guo, a geospatial data scientist with a BSc in Geography from Chuzhou University, China. My research interests include GIS, GeoAI, spatiotemporal data analysis, and environmental modeling. I have hands-on experience in research and projects involving remote sensing, spatial-temporal analysis, and environmental simulation.

I am passionate about leveraging GIScience, computer science, and data-driven methods (machine learning, GeoAI, spatial-temporal data mining) to address real-world geospatial challenges and support decision-making.
