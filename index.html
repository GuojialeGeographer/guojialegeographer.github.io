Jiale Guo
M.S. in Geoinformatics Engineering
Politecnico di Milano, Italy

I am an interdisciplinary researcher fascinated by the intersection of geography, computer science, and data analytics. My research journey began with the ancient Chinese wisdom: "The four directions and the upper and lower regions make up the universe, and the past and future and the present combine to create infinity" - a philosophical perspective that beautifully captures the essence of "space-time" in geographic studies. 我是一名跨学科研究者，致力于地理学、计算机科学和数据分析的交叉领域研究。我的研究旅程始于中国古代智慧："四方上下曰宇，往古来今曰宙"——这一哲学观点完美地捕捉了地理研究中"时空"的本质。

My research interests focus on applying GIScience, computer science, and data-driven methods (machine learning, GeoAI, spatial-temporal data mining) to address real-world geospatial challenges. I am particularly passionate about studying human-environment interactions and seeking pathways to harmonious coexistence through the innovative application of geospatial technologies. 我的研究兴趣集中在应用地理信息科学、计算机科学和数据驱动方法（机器学习、地理人工智能、时空数据挖掘）解决现实世界中的地理空间挑战。我特别热衷于研究人类与环境的交互作用，并通过地理空间技术的创新应用寻求和谐共存的途径。

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Geoinformatics Engineering at the Politecnico di Milano, Italy, with an expected graduation in 2026. Prior to this, I obtained a Bachelor degree in Geography from Chuzhou University, China, where I led research on spatial-temporal pattern mining of PM2.5 pollution using 3D GIS technology and spatial-temporal cube modeling. 目前，我正在意大利米兰理工大学攻读地理信息工程硕士学位，预计2026年毕业。在此之前，我在中国滁州学院获得了地理学学士学位，在那里我领导了使用3D GIS技术和时空立方体模型对PM2.5污染进行时空模式挖掘的研究。

My professional experience includes working as a research assistant at the Northwest Institute of Eco-Environment and Resources, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I contributed to environmental modeling projects using the SWAT model for studying land use impacts on surface runoff in Qinghai Lake. Through these experiences, I've developed expertise in geographic modeling, simulation, data analysis, and visualization. 我的专业经验包括在中国科学院西北生态环境资源研究院担任研究助理，在那里我使用SWAT模型参与环境建模项目，研究土地利用对青海湖表面径流的影响。通过这些经验，我在地理建模、模拟、数据分析和可视化方面积累了专业知识。

I am looking for collaborators 我正在寻找合作伙伴 interested in GeoAI, Spatial Analysis, and their applications in environmental science, urban planning, and sustainable development. My goal is to develop innovative methodologies that bridge theoretical approaches from GIScience with practical applications that support decision-making. If you are interested in collaborating, please feel free to reach out via email. 对地理人工智能、空间分析及其在环境科学、城市规划和可持续发展中的应用感兴趣的合作者。我的目标是开发创新方法，将地理信息科学的理论方法与支持决策的实际应用相结合。如果您有兴趣合作，请随时通过电子邮件联系我。
